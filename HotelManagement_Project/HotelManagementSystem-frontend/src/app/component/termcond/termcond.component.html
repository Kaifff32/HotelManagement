1. Reservations must be made through one of the Hotel Blue official websites.

2. The Lower Rate must be for the same type of accommodation, which means the same room type, at the same hotel or resort, with the same dates and length of stay, same number of guests, with a similar view or room size, same designation as either cancellable or non-cancellable, same advance purchase policies and the same terms and conditions governing the room rate.

3. You must complete and submit a claim submittal web form to Hotel Blue in accordance with the claim form’s instructions to process the claim.

4. You must submit your claim within 24 hours of the reservation being made with Hotel Blue and at least 48 hours (72 hours for non-English Hotel Blue websites) before the standard check-in time at the applicable Hotel Blue hotel or resort.

6. Rate comparisons will be made net of taxes, gratuities, service charges, early departure fees, or other fees and incidental charges. Free breakfast and free or discounted amenities or services offered as part of the Lower Rate will be excluded from the rate comparison, and will not be provided by Four Seasons when matching the Lower Rate.
